Treading with Tunes

A study conducted on the effects of music on oneâ€™s walking speed

Participants

Two avid enjoyers of various types of music, and frequent walkers. One of us is a lot taller than the other, resulting in more nuanced data. We both recorded each song we listened to while walking, along with each songâ€™s corresponding genre and BPM.

Data Collection

Data was collected using the iOS Health app for walking speed and stride information, and the Last.fm app for music listening history. We collected data over the course of 2 months.

Lets get some terminology out of the way!

Genre

Genre refers to the main category of music. While numerous musical pieces may belong to multiple genres or even have various sub-genres, they typically possess a primary genre that defines their overarching style.

EX: As It Was by Harry Styles - Pop

BPM

BPM, also known as beats per minute, is a measure of how fast the musicâ€™s tempo is. The larger the number, song usually have a faster tempo. However, some slow songs have a very fast BPM, this is because when the BPM is too fast, you can sync to half of the BPM.

EX: As It Was by Harry Styles - 174
